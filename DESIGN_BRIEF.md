## üé® Design Philosophy

### Core Principles

**1. Cinematic Luxury**
- Full-bleed imagery that transports viewers
- Atmospheric photography with moody, contemplative tones
- Video-like quality in static images
- Cinematic aspect ratios and compositions

**2. Quiet Confidence**
- Restrained UI that doesn't compete with content
- Minimal chrome and interface elements
- Let the properties and photography speak for themselves
- Whisper, don't shout

**3. Editorial Sophistication**
- Magazine-quality layouts
- Generous negative space (white space, but dark)
- Asymmetrical grids for visual interest
- Thoughtful typography hierarchy

**4. Emotional Resonance**
- Evocative, sensory-rich copy
- Imagery that tells stories
- Focus on experience over features
- Appeal to desires and aspirations

**5. Timeless Elegance**
- Classic design patterns with modern execution
- Avoid trendy elements that will date quickly
- Premium materials metaphor (silk, velvet, aged leather)
- Heritage meets contemporary

## ‚ú® Interactions & Animations

### Animation Principles

1. **Subtle & Refined** - Never over-animate
2. **Purposeful** - Every animation communicates something
3. **Smooth Easing** - Use `ease-out` for most transitions
4. **Performant** - Only animate transform and opacity
5. **Respectful** - Consider reduced motion preferences

### Animation Timings

```css
Fast:     200ms   (micro-interactions)
Standard: 300ms   (hover states)
Smooth:   400ms   (image scales)
Slow:     600ms   (fade-ins, reveals)
Cinematic: 800ms  (hero animations)
```

### Scroll-Based Animations

**Fade Up Pattern:**
- Initial: opacity 0, translateY 20px
- Animate: opacity 1, translateY 0
- Trigger: Element 30% in viewport
- Duration: 600ms
- Easing: ease-out
- Stagger: 200ms delay between siblings

**Example sections:**
- Brand introduction
- Section headlines
- Property cards
- Article cards

### Hover States

**Images:**
- Scale: 1 ‚Üí 1.05
- Duration: 400ms
- Easing: ease-out
- Overflow: hidden on container

**Cards:**
- TranslateY: 0 ‚Üí -8px
- Duration: 300ms
- Easing: ease-out
- Shadow: Subtle lift (optional)

**Buttons:**
- Background: transparent ‚Üí filled
- Color: accent ‚Üí contrast
- Duration: 300ms
- Easing: ease-out

**Links:**
- Color: initial ‚Üí accent
- Duration: 200ms
- Easing: ease-out

**Arrows:**
- TranslateX: 0 ‚Üí 4px
- Duration: 200ms
- Trigger: Parent hover

### Parallax Effects

**Hero Section:**
- Scroll multiplier: 0.5x
- Effect: Background image moves slower than scroll
- Implementation: Transform translateY based on scrollY
- Smooth: RequestAnimationFrame for 60fps

### Menu Animations

**Desktop:**
- Nav bar: Slide down from top on load (600ms)
- Background fade on scroll (300ms)

**Mobile Overlay:**
- Overlay fade in (300ms)
- Menu items stagger in (100ms delay each)
- Divider line scale X (500ms)
- Social icons fade in last (400ms)

### Scroll Indicators

**Chevron Bounce:**
- Animation: TranslateY 0 ‚Üí 8px ‚Üí 0
- Duration: 1500ms
- Repeat: Infinite
- Easing: ease-in-out

**Progress Dots:**
- Active: Champagne Gold, 8px circle
- Inactive: Graphite, 8px circle
- Transition: 300ms

## üì∏ Photography Guidelines

### Image Style

**Mood:**
- Atmospheric, moody, contemplative
- Golden hour / blue hour lighting preferred
- Soft, diffused light (never harsh)
- Candlelit interiors
- Misty landscapes

**Composition:**
- Cinematic aspect ratios (16:9, 2.35:1)
- Generous negative space
- Leading lines and depth
- Foreground interest
- Rule of thirds

**Color Grading:**
- Desaturated, muted palette
- Warm shadows, cool highlights
- Increased contrast
- Film-like grain (subtle)
- Avoid overly vibrant colors

**Subject Matter:**
- Interiors: Show lifestyle, not catalog shots
- Include people occasionally (candid, not posed)
- Detail shots (textures, materials, craftsmanship)
- Establishing shots (neighborhood, landscape)
- Experiential moments (dining, relaxing, sunset)

### Technical Specs

- Format: JPEG or WebP
- Resolution: Minimum 2400px wide
- Aspect ratios: 16:9, 1:1, 2:3 depending on placement
- File size: Optimized, max 300KB per image
- Color space: sRGB

### Image Treatment

**Always apply:**
- Dark overlay (30-60% black opacity)
- Gradient overlay on text areas (bottom fade)
- Slightly reduced saturation (-10%)
- Slightly increased contrast (+10%)

**Hero images:**
- 120vh height to allow parallax
- 50% black overlay
- Gradient from bottom (Obsidian 100% ‚Üí transparent)

**Card images:**
- Exact dimensions for art direction
- Hover scale transform (scale 1.05)
- Overflow hidden on container

### Keyboard Navigation

- All interactive elements focusable

- Remove infinite animations

## üìù Content Guidelines

### Voice & Tone

**Voice (consistent):**
- Sophisticated but not pretentious
- Confident but not arrogant
- Knowledgeable but not lecturing
- Warm but not casual

**Tone (varies by context):**
- Hero: Aspirational, poetic
- Body copy: Informative, inviting
- CTAs: Clear, encouraging
- Errors: Helpful, understanding

### Writing Style

**Do:**
- Use sensory language (what guests will see, feel, taste)
- Write in active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Use specific details over generic claims
- Paint pictures with words
- Show, don't tell

**Don't:**
- Use clich√©s ("luxury redefined", "world-class")
- Over-use adjectives (one strong word > three weak ones)
- Write in passive voice
- Use jargon or industry speak
- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Be overly formal or stuffy

### Copy Examples

**Good:**
- "Watch sunrise paint the lavender fields gold from your private terrace"
- "Cobblestone streets and hidden garden squares await your afternoon wanderings"
- "Three hundred years of history, reimagined for today's discerning traveler"

**Avoid:**
- "Experience luxury like never before"
- "World-class amenities and unparalleled service"
- "Your dream vacation awaits"
